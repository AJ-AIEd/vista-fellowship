The Diagnostic Question
"If AI completed this task perfectly, what learning would disappear?"
⬛ When everything meaningful disappearsAI can complete the task instead of the student. The design has a problem.
🟩 When nothing significant disappearsThe task protects genuine learning. The design is working.
Step 1
Initial Audit
Upload your materials and work through this alone first. Simple, focused, accessible. Your honest baseline — before any AI or conversation.
Step 2
AI Stress-Test
Return here after your conversation with Cajina. Use AI as a thinking partner and model the process we want students to develop.
Step 3
Final Audit
Complete this at the end of a full cycle. Deeper reflection, real tensions, evidence of how your thinking has changed. For when you are ready to go deep.
Work through the steps in order. Return here any time.
